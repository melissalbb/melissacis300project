About Us

Our Story

Our journey began with a shared love for the South of France — a region that stole our hearts with its golden light, sea breeze, and timeless charm. From the cobbled streets of Aix-en-Provence to the lavender fields of Valensole, we discovered a way of life worth sharing.

What started as a small travel blog has grown into a full guide helping travelers uncover the true soul of the Côte d’Azur and Provence.

Who We Are

We are a group of passionate travelers, storytellers, and explorers with a love for the South of France. From the glamorous Riviera to the quiet lavender fields of Provence, we capture the essence of this breathtaking region by combining culture, history, cuisine, and hidden gems into one curated guide.

What We Do

We craft personalized itineraries, design road trips, and recommend hidden gems that bring the South of France to life. Whether you dream of luxury escapes in Saint-Tropez or rustic markets in Provence, our guides help you experience every flavor and color of this beautiful region.

Our Promise

Travel should be unforgettable and effortless. That’s why our platform helps you plan journeys that balance discovery and relaxation. With our Trip Planner, you can design your perfect itinerary or choose from carefully curated routes filled with charm, beauty, and culture.

Our Vision

We believe travel should connect people to place, culture, and themselves. Our mission is to make the South of France accessible and meaningful to every traveler. From art to vineyards, from beaches to mountain villages — we bring the Mediterranean lifestyle to your journey.
