Destinations

Nice

Nice, the capital of the French Riviera, blends Mediterranean charm with artistic heritage. From the Promenade des Anglais to the colorful Old Town, it’s a paradise for beach lovers and art enthusiasts alike. Visit the Matisse Museum, taste local specialties like Socca, and admire the sunset over the Baie des Anges.

Marseille

France’s oldest city, Marseille, offers a vibrant mix of cultures and stunning seaside views. Explore the Old Port, wander through the narrow alleys of Le Panier, and don’t miss the Calanques National Park for breathtaking hikes and turquoise coves. Its lively markets and fresh seafood make it a must for food lovers.

Cannes

Known for its world-famous Film Festival, Cannes is synonymous with glamour and elegance. Stroll along La Croisette, relax at golden beaches, or take a ferry to the peaceful Îles de Lérins. With luxury boutiques, fine dining, and cinematic charm, Cannes captures the essence of the Riviera lifestyle.

Monaco

The glamorous principality of Monaco is famed for its luxury, casinos, and panoramic views of the Mediterranean. Visit the Prince’s Palace, the Oceanographic Museum, or watch the Formula 1 Grand Prix from a café terrace. Despite its small size, Monaco’s elegance and energy make it unforgettable.

Saint-Tropez

Once a quiet fishing village, Saint-Tropez has become an icon of French chic. Famous for its golden beaches, stylish boutiques, and lively nightlife, it remains effortlessly charming. Stroll through the old port, enjoy a Tarte Tropézienne at a café, or dance the night away by the sea.

Aix-en-Provence

Aix-en-Provence is a city of fountains, art, and light. Birthplace of painter Paul Cézanne, it’s filled with history and beauty. Walk along Cours Mirabeau under plane trees, visit colorful markets, and explore nearby vineyards. Every corner feels like a painting come to life.

Avignon

Avignon, the City of Popes, is a medieval masterpiece. Discover the majestic Palais des Papes, walk across the famous Pont d’Avignon, and enjoy summer festivals filled with art and theater. Its narrow cobbled streets and historic charm make it a step back in time.

Antibes

Antibes offers the perfect blend of relaxation and culture. Visit the Picasso Museum, walk along the ramparts with views of the sea, and browse the bustling Provençal market. With sandy beaches and a charming old town, it’s a Riviera favorite for travelers seeking authenticity and beauty.
